Welcome and thanks for visiting!

I am a PhD Candidate in Social Data Science at the University of Oxford, as an ESRC and Nuffield College Scholar.

My research focuses on political public opinion and polarisation, using machine learning, natural language processing, networks, and time-series.

Alongside my PhD, I collaborate with Oxford’s Department of Politics (AuthLIB project) and Science Po’s Centre for European Studies and Comparative Politics.

I hold an MPhil in Politics (Oxford, 2021) and a BA in Politics and International Relations (Exeter, 2019).
